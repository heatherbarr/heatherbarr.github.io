Kpop Events in the Area

Sweet Moments Ktown Webster Hall Mochi Dolci Kpop Nara Radio City Oh K Dog

Sweet Moments

Sweet Moments is a cute cafe nestled in heart of China town with kpop cupsleeve events almost every weekend during the summer. COme sip some refreshing drinks and grab a sweet treat while you mingle with other kpop fans.

Ktown

Koreatown is the go to spot in midtown for anything and everything related to Korea. Great restaurants and cafes like Jongro and Grace Street to get a taste of Korea. It also has the famous Koryo Bookstore which is the oldest Kpop album store in nyc

Websterhall

My absolute favorite concert venue in nyc which is a small intimate setting where I have seen multiple of my favorite artists up close. SInce it is only a standing room, you have to show up early in order to be in the front of the crowd. It is totally worth the wait to have such a good view.

Mochi Dolci

A cafe that specializes in Mochi Donuts and has a speakeasy in the back where kpop cupsleeve events are often held.

Kpop Nara

Located in the subway station near Central Park, Kpop Nara is a small kpop store that occassionaly hosts fan meetings of smaller kpop groups and solo artists. This is the place to go for signed merchandise.

Radio City

Most people associate Radio City with the Rockettes, I however assosciate it with the place where I got to meet the kpop group Monsta X. As of recent, this is becoming a primariy stop for Kpop artists that are on the rise.

OhKdog

America loves its corn dogs, however Korea has taken this classic to a whole new level by using sweet rice flour and using potatoes and mozzerella to make a deliscious Korean Hot dog.This is the perfect place to experience a Kpop Cupsleeve event and Try one of Korea's most popular street foods.
